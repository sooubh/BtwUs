Our Privacy Promise

BtwUs is built on a simple, uncompromising premise: what happens between you and your partner belongs only to the two of you.

100% Offline & Private

The application does not use external accounts, user registration, or cloud hosting servers. Everything you create, write, or import is stored strictly on your local physical device in an encrypted sandbox.

We do not collect, view, monitor, track, store, or sell any of your chats, relationships, emotions, media, or database contents.

Data We Collect (And What We Don't)

A. Relational Content (Memories, Commitments, Chats)

You may choose to record memories, track promise streaks, log commitments, and import offline WhatsApp export logs. None of this content leaves your phone. It is directly parsed and saved within a secure local database (Drift/SQLite) which is protected by AES-256 encryption.

B. Media Attachments

Images, videos, documents, and voice recordings selected by you are saved in the app's isolated local subdirectory on your device. We do not index these files to any cloud servers.

C. Access Credentials

If you enable Biometric Authentication (Fingerprint or Face Unlock) or set a secure PIN, these verification queries are handled natively by your operating system. The app never intercepts, handles, or uploads your raw biometric credentials.

On-Device AI Engine

BtwUs employs intelligent search models, language identification, and entity extraction to automatically tag and catalog your memories (e.g., tagging sweet messages, fights, promises, or dates).

Zero Network Transmission

All semantic classification and text search indexing are computed directly on your phone using Google ML Kit local APIs. There are no API requests, telemetry streams, or network-bound payloads associated with the intelligence features of this application.

Integrations & Third-Party Disclosures

To support development and handle premium subscriptions securely, BtwUs integrates with Google Play services. These platforms collect specific, restricted data:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Displays banners/interstitials inside the free tier. AdMob collects standard device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID) for ad delivery, targeting, and analytics. No personal relational context is ever shared with ad brokers.

Google Play Billing (IAP)

Processes subscriptions (Plus/Premium). Transaction logs, licenses, and product ID receipts are processed by the Google Play Store billing client to validate your subscription. We do not store or access your payment credentials.

Control, Deletion & Backups

You maintain total dominion over your archive at all times:

  • Local Purge: You can permanently wipe your entire record database and media cache at any time via Settings → Delete all data. This action is instantaneous and cannot be undone.
  • App Uninstallation: Uninstalling the BtwUs application from your Android phone will trigger the system to delete all local folders associated with BtwUs, resulting in absolute deletion.
  • Local Backup: You can export your data as an encrypted .btwus ZIP package locally. It is your responsibility to save and store this backup file securely, as we do not host backups on cloud servers.

Your Responsibilities & Data Care

Because BtwUs is a 100% offline app, it does not sync your data to any remote servers. This introduces specific ownership policies:

No Cloud Recovery

If your device is lost, stolen, formatted, severely damaged, or if you delete the app cache/files without having exported a backup, your memories are lost permanently. We cannot restore them because we never hold copies of your data.

Securing Your Access

You are solely responsible for setting strong access PINs and enabling biometrics if you want to keep others from opening the app on your physical device.

Offline Architecture

Your data. Your processor.

Most chat and journaling platforms store your private relationships on cloud servers, exposing them to hacks, data profiling, or company failures. BtwUs is built offline-first.

When you import a chat or snap a photo, processing happens exclusively inside your phone's sandbox. SQLCipher secures database files under hardware-level authentication. No external tracking, no accounts needed.
